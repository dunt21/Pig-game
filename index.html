RULES OF THE GAME 📜

1. Player 1 begins by clicking the "Roll Dice 🎲" button

2. If the dice shows 2-6, that number will be added to the active player's current score. If it shows 1, the current score of the active player resets to 0 their turn ends, and it's automatically switched to the next player.

3. Clicking on the "Hold 📥" button adds the active player's current score to their total score and the turn switches to the next player turns.

4. The player to reach or exceed 80 points wins the game 🎉.

5. Click on the "New Game" button to restart the game .
